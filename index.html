RBDC x ACM

List of projects that RBDC collabrate ACM.

Traffic Tracking

Hardness Level: 4

A Machine Learning application requested by the county to track the number and type of waste tracks passing through a certain intersection.

Living Wage calculator

Hardness Level: 3

Regional Economic Development for Eatsern Idaho (REDI) has asked the RBDC to build a Cost of Living Wage calculator for several states in the Intermountain West Region.

Chipantha Web Development

Hardness Level: 5

A local business started in Africa that designs and makes traditional hand made products. They need help in building a application to help them expend their business in the US.

Red Falcon Ranch Web Development

Hardness Level: 3

A local business located in Bonners Ferry, Idaho. The owners raise Angora Goats and sell mohair to make clothing such as hats and socks.

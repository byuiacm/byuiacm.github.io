TapTap

Project Info:

TapTap is an app designed to aid those with Special Needs. It was worked on during the BYU Idaho Hackathon and won the BYU Idaho ACM Chapter to win the "Most Polished" Award. Along with that achievement this app has won the attraction of the Los Angeles School District and waits in anticipation for the this app to arrive on the app store. This app is mainly designed for iOS and is still under construction.

Project Info:

Regional Economic Development for Eatsern Idaho (REDI) has asked the RBDC to build a Cost of Living Wage calculator for several states in the Intermountain West Region. This tool is to help individuals to be able to better understand what is the implication of moving and accepting a job in a new area. Essentially, this tool will tell someone whether their money is worth the same if they accept a job in a new area. There may be a potential for doing this for businesses.
